# Acceptable Use Policy

## Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) outlines the rules and expectations for using PressClass AI responsibly. The platform is intended strictly for educational and productivity purposes.

---

## Prohibited Activities

Users may not use PressClass AI to:

- Generate or distribute hate speech, harassment, or discriminatory content  
- Create sexually explicit material or content involving minors  
- Generate content that promotes self-harm, violence, or illegal activities  
- Engage in academic dishonesty, including submitting AI-generated work as original where prohibited by school policies  
- Attempt to reverse-engineer, exploit, or interfere with the platform’s systems  
- Upload malware, viruses, or malicious code

---

## Fair and Responsible Use

PressClass AI is designed to assist learning, not replace critical thinking or professional judgment. Users are expected to:

- Use AI-generated content as a support tool  
- Follow their institution’s academic integrity guidelines  
- Respect intellectual property rights

---

## Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in:

- Temporary account suspension  
- Permanent termination of access  
- Reporting to relevant authorities where required by law

PressClass AI reserves the right to take appropriate action to protect users and the platform.

---

This policy may be updated to reflect evolving legal, ethical, or technical considerations.
