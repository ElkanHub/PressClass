# Data & Content Ownership Policy

## Who Owns What?

This policy explains how content, data, and materials are owned, used, and managed within PressClass AI. Transparency around ownership is essential in educational environments, and PressClass AI is committed to respecting the rights of educators, students, and institutions.

---

## Your Content

You retain **full ownership** of all original content, documents, files, and data that you upload to PressClass AI. This includes, but is not limited to:

* Lesson outlines and teaching materials
* Notes, documents, and instructional resources
* Uploaded curriculum references or source files

In addition, you also retain ownership of the **specific AI-generated outputs** created for you through the platform, provided those outputs are based on your inputs and usage of PressClass AI.

PressClass AI does not claim ownership over your educational materials or intellectual property.

---

## How Your Content Is Used

To provide its services, PressClass AI must process, store, and display user content. By using the platform, you grant PressClass AI a **limited, non-exclusive, revocable license** to:

* Host your content securely
* Process content for AI-assisted generation and organization
* Display content within the app for your use

This license exists **solely for the purpose of delivering PressClass services** and does not permit PressClass AI to sell, publish, or redistribute your content without your explicit consent.

---

## AI Training and User Content

PressClass AI does **not use individual user content to train or fine-tune AI models** without clear permission or appropriate safeguards.

This ensures that your materials remain private and under your control.

---

## Data Portability and Export

PressClass AI supports data portability. You may export your content at any time in PDF (.pdf)

This allows you to retain independent copies of your work and integrate it into other tools or systems.

---

## Account Termination and Data Retention

If you choose to terminate your account, PressClass AI will handle your data responsibly.

Upon account termination:

* Your data will be scheduled for **deletion or anonymization**
* This process will occur after a defined retention period
Retention period: 30 days

This retention window allows users to recover data in case of accidental deletion while ensuring long-term privacy protection.

---

## Institutional Accounts

For schools or institutions using PressClass AI under organizational accounts, data ownership and management may be governed by additional agreements or institutional policies.

---

## Policy Updates

As PressClass AI evolves, this policy may be updated to reflect changes in features, legal requirements, or best practices. Users will be notified of material changes where required.

---

PressClass AI is committed to safeguarding user ownership, ensuring data portability, and maintaining trust in how educational content is handled.
